PRIVACY POLICY
Settle — Expense Splitting App
Last updated: March 2026

────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────

1. INTRODUCTION

Settle ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your personal
information. This Privacy Policy explains what data we collect, how we use it,
and your rights in relation to it.

By using the Settle app you agree to the practices described in this policy.

────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────

2. INFORMATION WE COLLECT

2.1 Account Information
  • Name and phone number (used to create and identify your account)
  • Profile photo (optional, uploaded by you)

2.2 Expense & Group Data
  • Groups you create or belong to
  • Expenses you add, edit, or delete
  • Split amounts and settlement records

2.3 Contacts (optional)
  • With your permission we access your device contacts to help you find friends
    who already use Settle, and to invite others.
  • We do not store your full contact list on our servers. Contact data is
    used only locally and to match against registered phone numbers.

2.4 Usage Data
  • Anonymised analytics events (e.g. "expense added", "group created") to
    understand how the app is used and improve features.
  • No personally identifiable information is attached to analytics events.

────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────

3. HOW WE USE YOUR INFORMATION

  • To provide and improve the Settle service
  • To sync your data across devices
  • To send transactional notifications (e.g. when someone adds an expense)
  • To detect and prevent fraud or abuse

We do NOT sell your personal data to third parties.

────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────

4. DATA STORAGE & SECURITY

Your data is stored securely using Supabase (PostgreSQL). All data in transit
is encrypted via TLS. We apply row-level security policies so users can only
access data they are authorised to see.

────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────

5. DATA RETENTION

  • Your account data is retained for as long as your account is active.
  • You may delete your account at any time; all associated data will be
    permanently removed within 30 days.

────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────

6. THIRD-PARTY SERVICES

Settle uses the following third-party services, each with their own privacy
policies:
  • Supabase — database and authentication (supabase.com/privacy)
  • Expo / React Native — app framework

────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────

7. CHILDREN'S PRIVACY

Settle is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data
from children.

────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────

8. CHANGES TO THIS POLICY

We may update this policy from time to time. Significant changes will be
communicated via an in-app notice. Continued use after changes constitutes
acceptance of the updated policy.
